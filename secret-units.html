Radiation Tank

R6Y radiation tank is one of the battlefield modification after obtaining the Soviet nuclear technology. Since the radiation shell couldn't be stored on the ammo rack inside the hull, the Allied choose to load it on an oscillating turret designed chassis.

  • Prerequisite:
    1. Allied Weapon Factory
    2. Allied Battle Command
  • Main Armament: 105mm T144
  • Ammunition: Armor Piercing
  • Cost: 2000
  • Trait: Radiation Shell

Siege Chopper

The Mi-2OV Siege Chopper comes after the Soviet obtaining aerodynamic statistic from the Allied. Armed with pod rocket launcher and 12.7mm machinegun, the siege chopper has both excellent mobility and fire power.

  • Prerequisite:
    1. Soviet Weapon Factory
    2. Soviet Radar
  • Main Armament: N/A
  • Cost: 2000
  • Trait: Siege

Tesla Assaulter

The Soviet design bureau has been trying to install a full powered tesla coil on a tank chassis. They digged the blueprint of outdated SU-100Y from a Soviet Battle Lab, and modify it into an assault gun.

  • Prerequisite:
    1. Soviet Weapon Factory
    2. Soviet Radar
  • Main Armament: N100T Capacitor
  • Ammunition: Electricity Stream
  • Cost: 2000
  • Trait: Release EMP
