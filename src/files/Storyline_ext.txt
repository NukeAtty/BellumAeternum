"Under the shadow of the eternal war, truth is hidden in the dark."


Storyline:

Background:
1951 - Loser of the Korean War
The United Nation Force cannot push further more to North Korea, while the Soviet Union has greatly increased its military force, and support the North Korea on battlefield.

1952 - Reshuffle
The Washington DC decided to reshuffle the United Nation Force to the Allied Force, with standardised arsenal and intel.

1953 - The Troop Vacuum 
When the Allied force supports Korean war, The western Europe was experiencing troop vacuum, and Soviet spies has reported to Moscow, then the Soviet leader Joseph Stalin has launched attack on West Germany. Although there are still Allied garrison, but since more advanced weapons has been sent to Korea, the remaining Allied troops has failed very quickly.
After the Soviet army entirely captured Germany, the Allied force realised a new war will break out in Europe, so they start to send most nuclear weapons to France. France has strong fortification, and the Allied believes that with nuclear weapons, the Soviets wouldn't dare to push further.

1954 - Crack the Ice
The Soviets launched a sudden attack at Metz, France, and wiped out the Allied force. Meanwhile, they have captured a battle command center, which loaded the entire Allied military deployment in Europe. After that, the Soviet intellegence agency co-operated with special forces, sabotaged all nuclear base in France. Without worrying about nuclear bombs, the Soviets easily captured Paris, and the Allied forces had to retreat to Great Britain from Dunkirk. 
However, the Allieds were committed to win the Korean war, and doesn't know much about what's happenning in Europe. Finally, the Allied forces has taken control of the entire Korean peninsula, and Chinese volunteer army has retreated to the border.
