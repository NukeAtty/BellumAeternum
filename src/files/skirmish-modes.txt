General Rules:
	1. Basic Infantry Only(E1/E2/FLAKT/GGI/ENGINEER/SENGINEER).
	2. Base destroyed loses.
	3. Credits comes from oil drills.
	4. In order to capture the oil drill, you need to defeat the guard.
	5. There are three dirlls on the map.
	6. There are 6 road guards (SHK) on the map.
	7. There are three towers on the map, capturing the tower autobuild a gate.
	8. Each tower has a guard vehicle, change sides upon destroyed.
	9. A juncture in the middle provides additional credits.
	10. All infantries gain self heal.

Frontline Mode:
	Sides:
		Attack
		Defense

	Attack:
		进攻方出生在下方，优势为前期部队较多。
		每用工程师占领一座基地，都会刷新进攻时间。
		初始进攻时间为10:00，如果占领一座基地，则加5:00。
		当所有前线基地都被进攻方占领后，防守方的炮塔会停电。

	Defense:
		防守方出生在上方，前期必须从前方点位的战车工厂造坦克，初始兵力无法及时赶到。
		防守方拥有4座炮塔，如果炮塔全部被摧毁则判定防守失败。
		倒计时结束时防守方获胜。

	Map:
		地图上会有补给点，可以给坦克加血。
		双方都有空降步兵、炮击、地雷和侦察机技能。
