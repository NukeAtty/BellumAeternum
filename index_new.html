What Makes Bellum Æternum More Realistic?

Enhanced Visuals

Graphics have been remastered. Flames, smoke, particles, debris, and wreckage are brilliantly integrated into the battlefield.

Realistic Design

All units are historically inspired, offering an authentic experience for military enthusiasts and war history fans.

Epic Campaign

Stunning storyline based on real history, an alternative version of collisions between two super powers.

Factions

ALLIED

Formed by the United States of America, the Allied forces are military group that combined military forces from North American states and Western European states. The scientific teams of the Allied Force has mastered optics theories, and applied them into stealth technology and prism technology.

ARSENAL

SOVIET

Formed by the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the Soviet forces, a.k.a. Red Army, are military group that combined power of Eastern European socialist states. The Soviet scientists are experts of electromagnetic and nuclear physics, and developed tesla technology and nuclear technology.
