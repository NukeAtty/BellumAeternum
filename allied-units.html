Grizzly Tank

M54 Grizzly tank is the successor of the Allied new generation battle tank. Armed with 90mm M5 cannon, the M54 Grizzly has decent armor and fair rate of fire. Overall, Grizzly is tough and sturdy and with good fire power.

IFV

The M21 IFV is one of the best multi-purpose armored vehicle in Allied Force, armed with 6-barrel missile launcher, the M21 IFV is capable of anti-air and anti-armor. Additionally, M21 IFV can easily swap its weapon depends on operators, and this makes it the best support on the battlefield.

  • Main Armament: AIM 6X Missile Launcher
  • Ammunition: High Explosive
  • Alpha Damage: 120 * 6
  • Reload Time: 12s
  • Cost: 800
  • Special Purpose: Crew Changes Weapon

Stealth Tank

  • Main Armament: 105mm Recoilless Rifle M45

  • Alpha Damage: 320 * 4
  • Reload Time: 22s
  • Cost: 1600
  • Special Purpose: Stealth when stand still

Prism Tank

  • Main Armament: Prism Caster

  • Alpha Damage: 1050
  • Reload Time: 20s
  • Cost: 1800

Battle Fortress

The T111 Battle Fortress is the ultimate weapon of the Allied Force, it armed with destructive 155mm cannon and can destroy most enemy in one shot. Because of its decent armor, it is tough enough to push all the way to enemy base, and this make T111 the best assault tank.

  • Main Armament: 155mm T11
  • Ammunition: Armor Piercing
  • Alpha Damage: 750
  • Reload Time: 12s
  • Cost: 3000
  • Special Purpose: Can crush light vehicles
