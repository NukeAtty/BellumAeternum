Tesla Tank

Apocalypse Tank

The KV-550 "Apocalypse" is the ultimate successor of the Kliment Voroshilov Series, one of the most famous Soviet heavy tank production line. The heavy armored Apocalypse armed with twin barrel cannon and MLRS, playing the role of the superior devastator.

  • Main Armament: 122mm BL-9X
  • Secondary Armament: Apocalypse MLRS
  • Alpha Damage: 390
  • Reload Time: 8s
  • Cost: 3000
  • Special Purpose: Can crush light vehicles
