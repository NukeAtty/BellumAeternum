Specific Progress

Coding:

All presented units has finished INI coding, however, the final stats could still be adjusted for the sake of balance.

Voxels:

Voxels are graphics for vehicles. Land vehicles have almost finished. Aircrafts are still working in progress. All civilian vehicles are also working in progress since they will be all replaced.

SHPs:

SHPs are graphics for buildings and infantries. Infantries will not be change too much, and there are several new buildings for both factions working in progress.

Maps:

New maps and missions have just started, later will introduce the first skirmish map, the Verdant Hills.

Sound:

Right now sound is at a lower priority, since the main content isn't really finished. It will be started right after maps.

Engines Empowered the Mod

Ares

Ares is the next generation of binary-based Command & Conquer modding. To successfully run Bellum Aeternum, the Ares version 3.0 should be installed first.

Ares Official Website

Installation Guide

Step 1. Make sure you got the client of Red Alert 2 Yuri's Revenge version 1.001. You can purchase the C&C Ultimate Collection.

Step 2. Download Ares 3.0 and unzip the file, then copy and paste these files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge.

Step 3. Download Phobos 3.0 and unzip the file, then copy and paste these files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge.

Step 4. Download Bellum Aeternum and unzip the file, then copy and paste all files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge

Step 5. Double Click [RunAres.bat] to run the game.
