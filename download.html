Installation Guide

Step 1. Make sure you've got an unmodded client of Red Alert 2 Yuri's Revenge version 1.001. You can purchase the C&C Ultimate Collection, or purchase from Steam.

Step 2. Download Bellum Æternum and unzip the file.

Step 3. Copy and paste the unzipped files into the root directory of your Red Alert 2 Yuri's Revenge 1.001 folder.

Step 4. Double click the BELauncher.exe to start the game.
