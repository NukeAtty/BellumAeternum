Specific Progress

■ Coding:

Rules coding of presented units are fully accomplished, while some hidden units are still working in progress.

AI has been reworked, and is 80% finished, and is experiencing a plenty of local testing before publish.

■ Voxels:

Voxels are graphics for vehicles.

All presented vehicles are finished, and hidden/civilian vehicles are still working in progress.

■ SHPs:

SHPs are graphics for buildings and infantries. Infantries will not be change too much, and there are several new buildings working in progress.

■ Maps:

New skirmish maps: Both Verdant Hills(2) and Leavenworth(2-4) has been completed.

The first mission of Allied campaign is finished, the second mission is still polishing. The story line and scripts of the first season campaign has finished.

■ Sound:

Right now sound is at a lower priority, since the main content isn't really finished. It will be started right after maps.

Engines Empowered the Mod

Ares

Ares is the next generation of binary-based Command & Conquer modding. To successfully run Bellum Aeternum, the Ares version 3.0 should be installed first.

Ares Official Website

Phobos

Phobos is a community engine extension project providing a set of new features and fixes for Yuri’s Revenge. The Phobos version 3.0.1 should be installed first.

Phobos Github Pages

Installation Guide

Step 1. Make sure you got the client of Red Alert 2 Yuri's Revenge version 1.001. You can purchase the C&C Ultimate Collection.

Step 2. Download Ares 3.0 and unzip the file, then copy and paste these files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge.

Step 3. Download Phobos 3.0.1 and unzip the file, then copy and paste these files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge.

Step 4. Download Bellum Aeternum and unzip the file, then copy and paste all files into the folder of your Yuri's Revenge

Step 5. Double Click [RunAres.bat] to run the game.
