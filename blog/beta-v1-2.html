Bellum Æternum Beta v1.2 Devlog

Last Update: 06/15/2025

Navigation

Players had given tons of feedbacks since beta v1 of Bellum Æternum releases, and now the beta v1.2 is releasing in order to fix those problems. Besides that, there are more content updated in Bellum Æternum Beta v1.2.

All changes will be presented in this devlog, and you can download the newest version in the Official Download page.

What's New

Brand New Client

Since a lot of players are using laptop, some of them with lower resolution, the new client now supports lower resolution, and players can adjust client resolution in the settings panel.

New Units

Civilian Vehicle

Metro has been added to the game, could be the first subway/underground in Red Alert 2.

New Buildings

Old Allied Buildings

Classic Allied buildings has been added to the game, you can see them in the prologue of campaign missions.

Civilian Buildings

Added several new civilian buildings

New Game Mode

Siege

PVP special. Players are grouped in attacker and defender. Attacker need to destroy all defenders or destroy the special target before the countdown ends. Defenders need to destroy all attackers or protect the special target until countdown ends. Both sides can only use basic troops and vehicles.

New Maps

Sturmburg (Siege)

The first siege map. 2 players as defender and 6 players as attacker. Defenders are born with paratrooper and 4 tech oil derrick and a machine shop nearby.

Modern Times

Modern Times is an 8 players map, which supports standard mode, team alliance and meat grinder.

Heck Freezes Over

Heck Freezes Over is a classic vanilla 8 players skirmish map. Now, it has returned with optimized AI performance and new graphics.

Enhancements

Graphics

New Terrain

Terrain tiles of new urban has been reworked. Compare to the vanilla version, the new version looks more old fashioned.

Effects

  • Trailer of tank gun shell has been adjusted.
  • Trailer of rocket launchers has been reworked.
  • Slimmer rifle trailer.
  • Electric arc of Tesla trooper and tesla tank for overpowering a tesla coil has changed their color.
  • Smaller rifle bullet animation.

Models

Several buildings has added snow version.

Remastered model for Machine Shop.

Interface

Changed loading screen for Allied and Soviet campaign.

Changed loading screen for observer.

Campaign

Campaign has been optimized for new version, including terrain and buildings.

Rebalance

Units

  • Slightly increased damage for flak track.
  • Now, one single tesla tank can power a tesla coil.

Special Tactics

Increased damage of Nuke Attack.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed the problem that Nuke Attack couldn't rocker light vehicles off.
  • Fixed muzzle flash coordinate of some civilian buildings.
  • Fixed damage flame coordinate of some civilian buildings.
  • Fixed muzzle flash coordinate of some units.
