Bellum Æternum：β v1.1 Devlog

Last Update: 2025/05/02

Navigation

Players had given tons of feedbacks since beta v1 of Bellum Æternum releases, and now the beta v1.1 is releasing in order to fix those problems. Besides that, there are more content updated in Bellum Æternum Beta v1.1.

All changes will be presented in this devlog, and you can download the newest version in the Official Download page.

What's New?

New Units

Added new vehicle, Centurion Tank, mission only.

Added new vehicle, Soviet Armoured Transport, mission only.

Added new vehicle, Black Prince AVRE, mission only.

Added new aircraft, Ilyushin Bomber, mission only.

New Buildings

Added some new civilian European style country house.

New Map

New map: Corniche (2-2)

Enhancements

Unit Change

Commando now has new mechanic for C4 bomb. One commando can carry 3 C4s, C4 bombs won't explode at once, but takes couple of seconds. Once all C4 used up, commando will need to waiting for 30 seconds to "reload".

Voiceover

Changed voiceover of the Tesla Assaulter.

Graphic

Added snow theatre for some civilian buildings.

Remade model for the civilian hospital.

Changed color for Kirov Airship, now it won't be over-exposed.

Remade loading screens for both skirmish and campaign missions.

Maps

Optimised prologue of campaign missions.

Optimised these skirmish maps:

  • Verdant Hills
  • Leavenworth

Rebalance

Katyusha Rocket Artillery has increased damage dealt to infantries and vehicles, and increased its area of effect.

Patton Tank (mission only) has increased penetration rate.

Flak Track has changed its main armament from 23mm AA gun to 14.5 KPVT machinegun, decreased damage to heavy armoured vehicles.

Bug Fixes

Steam Version Supported

Fixed the bug when this mod is installed on Steam version of Yuri's Revenge, it cannot enter the game.

Power Plant Explosion Adjust

The explosion range caused by a destroyed power plant has been proven to be too big, so now it has changed to a smaller area of effect.

Miscellaneous

    Fixed Soviet Weapon Factory from incorrect strength.
    Fixed Soviet Radar from incorrect armour.
