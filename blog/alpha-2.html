Bellum Æternum Second Alpha Test

Last Update: 11/10/2024

Navigation

BUG Fixes Rebalances Enhancements New Things Future Updates

The second version of Bellum Æternum Alpha will come out soon, and here we will go through some information about all changes to the early access version.

Be aware that this will be the last English version of alpha test, because all version will be integrated into new client in later development.

BUG Fixes

  1. Fixed the problem of snipers couldn't clear occupants.
  2. Fixed Intruder's firing coordinate.
  3. Fixed the error caused by the Radiation tank.
  4. Fixed upgraded units has invalid sight improvement.
  5. Fixed the graphical bug when Dawnhawk Transport is destroyed.

Rebalances

Tesla Trooper

The Tesla Troopers are now deals lower damage to buildings, and the damage deal to defense buildings is unchanged.

Tesla Tank

The Tesla Tank has lowered its DPM(Damage per minutes), and can fire while moving, also increased its hit point from 1300 to 1550.

Rhino Tank

The Rhino Tank has increased acceleration and hull rotating speed.

I.F.V.

The I.F.V. has increaed its maximum speed and acceleration, and increased the burst deley.

Flak Track

The Flak Track has increased its maximum speed and acceleration.

Prism Tank

The Prism Tank has increased its acceleration.

Dogs

Dogs has increased hit point due to the longer range of infantries.

Apocalypse Tank

The Apocalypse Tank has decreaed its DPM and acceleration.

Tesla Assaulter

The Tesla Assaulter has abolished its EM Pulse releasing skill, and gives primary weapon a conducting effect.

Siege Chopper

The Siege Chopper has increased damage and the range of rocket pod.

Mobile Radar

The Mobile Radar has canceled its weapon, but lowered the price to 1600.

Dawnhawk Transport

The Dawnhawk Transport is now untrainable, but passengers can still gain experience from firing.

Sight Adjustment

Conscript, RPG Trooper, Assaulter, Desolator, Battle Fortress and Siege Chopper have increased sight.

Spy, Stealth Tank, Radiation Tank and Stealth APC have decreased sight.

Miscellaneous

Intruder's napalm has fixed burning damage too low to buildings problem.

Decreased the firepower and armor buff from crates.

Fixed the problem of building time too long for some units.

Enhancements

  1. Flak Track has fixed black spot on hull.
  2. Grizzly Tank has changed its model and icon.
  3. Rhino Tank has changed its model and icon.
  4. Added upgraded icon for all trainable units.
  5. All voxel units has changed light source.
  6. Changed skirmish loading screen image.
  7. Soviet Intelligence Agency has changed model and icon.

New Things

  1. Added mission-only unit: Grizzly Tank (Arctic)
  2. Added mission-only units: Rhino Tank (Prototype)
  3. Added a new skirmish map: Frost Reach (2-4)

Future Updates

  1. Campaign: There are 7 missions planned for each side.
  2. CnCNet Client: There will be CnCNet-compatible client in the future.
  3. More Skirmish Maps: There will be more skirmish map with multiple game modes in the future.
  4. More Tech Buildings: Most tech buildings from Vanilla Yuri's Revenge is going to remake, and there will be more tech building types.
  5. More Hidden Technology: There will be more hidden units to gain from crates and secret labs.

FAQ

On its MODDB page.

The Beta version will be available when all campaign missions finished.

The CnCNet client will be integrated in the first Beta version.
