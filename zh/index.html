万世之战有什么特点？

重制单位

旧的单位全部重制，并被赋予新的机制和玩法。现在的单位拥有更加战术性的操作感以及更加突出的战场定位。

画质提升

弹道，爆炸效果，残骸，爆炸碎片，可动履带等细节的加入使得整个游戏的画质提升到了一个更高的境界。

宏大叙事

故事线进行了重新布局，讲述了上世纪60年代美苏两个超级大国的争端与冲突，战火之中只有强者才能生存。

对战平衡

遭遇战模式已经进行了再平衡，你将不再可能爆兵，同时简单的突进也无法解决问题，战术和战略才是王道。

美苏争霸从未停止，相反的，一切才刚刚开始。

苏联

The Soviet Red Army is directly lead by the Soviet Union, cooperates with armed forces of other socialist states such as Eastern European Union and Confederation of South America. Note that the People's Republic of China was previously in the Soviet Red Treaty, but after the Soviet Union invaded its northern teritory, China quit the treaty and becomes neutral. The Soviet is mainly using tesla technology and nuclear technology, which profoundly improved the energy supply and weapon power.

盟军

The Allied Force is formed by multiple North American and Western European states including the USA, Canada, UK, France and West Germany. The Allied lost its territory by over engaging Korean War and vacuuming its European defense. Only the Great Britain survived from the Soviet invasion. Under the pressure of the Soviet tank flood, the Allied has developed stealth technology and prism technology to prevent destruction from the Soviet Blitzkrieg.
