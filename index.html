Factions

The conflict between Allied and Soviet has never end, instead, it's just a begin.

Soviet

The Soviet Red Army is directly lead by the Soviet Union, cooperates with armed forces of other socialist states. When the Allied forces are engaging the Korea peninsula, the Soviet Army takes the chance and invades the western Europe. The Soviet is mainly using tesla technology and nuclear technology, which profoundly improved the energy supply and weapon power.

Soviet Info

Allied

The Allied Force is formed by multiple North American and Western European states. The Allied lost its territory by over engaging Korean War and vacuuming its European defense. Only the Great Britain survived from the Soviet invasion. Under the pressure of the Soviet armour flood, the Allied has developed stealth technology and prism technology to prevent destruction from the Soviet Blitzkrieg.
