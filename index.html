What Makes the Mod Realistic?

Reasonable Size

All units are now in a reasonable size, that the infantry won't be too big, and structures won't be too small.

Enhanced Effects

Flame, smoke, trail, debris, wreckages. A real battlefield will be shown.

Realistic Design

All units are within the scale of the early Cold war era, with very limited Sci-fi nonsense.

Proper Rhythm

Battle rhythm is slower and easier to handle than before, but requires more strategies and tactics.
