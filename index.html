What Makes Bellum Æternum More Realistic?

Enhanced Visuals

Graphics have been remastered. Flames, smoke, particles, debris, and wreckage are brilliantly integrated into the battlefield.

Realistic Design

All units are historically inspired, offering an authentic experience for military enthusiasts and war history fans.

The dimensions of all units have been strictly standardized, ensuring all elements now maintain realistic proportional scaling.
